    Financial Department Manager

    We are looking for a reliable Financial Manager that will analyze every day Financial activities and
    subsequently provide advice and guidance to upper management on future Financial plans.

    The goal is to enable the company's leaders to make sound business decisions and meet the
    company's objectives.

    Responsibilities

    Assist with response to inquiries, providing data quickly and accurately, using mostly sql, but also
    excel and other tools such as tableau

    Provide financial reports and interpret financial information to managerial staff while recommending
    future courses of action.

    Analyze costs, pricing, variable cpntributions, sales results and the company's actual performanace
    compared to the business plans.

    Deep data analysis both on the supply and demand side to provide a clear understand on the
    marketplace performanace at different levels.

    Oversee operations of the finance department, set goals and objectives, and dedign a framework for
    these to be met.

    Assist with the production of reports, and gather external data to have a better understanding on
    out performanace vs the industry.

    Requirements

    Proven experience as a Financial Manager.

    Bachelor of Science degree in Business Informatics, Information Systems, Business Administration
    or related fiels.

    Expertise in SQL and experience with large databases and raw data, including the ability to write
    queries against large werehouses.

    Extensive understanding of financial trends both within the company and general market.

    Ability to work proactively and result driven: always trying to learn that can help
    you take the most accurate decision.

    Strond spoken and written English is required. Spanish and Italian knowledge would be a plus.
